Facebook Lite

Facebook Lite is an app by Facebook which includes similar features with less technical requirements. Android users with limited data, poor connections, or older phones can still enjoy using Facebook to connect with friends, family, and businesses through their phone.The Facebook Lite app is small, allowing you to save space on your phone and use Facebook in 2G conditions.
